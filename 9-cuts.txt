1. Standard Shot - End of One / Start of another (One clip - followed by another clip)
2. Jump Cut
3. J Cut
4. L Cut  - Conversational scenes
5. Cutting on action - very common - head turns, hand movements, kick, 
6. Cross-cutting - Parallel editing for building suspense
7. Cutaway - Inform user where you are in scene
8. Montage - Sequences / Rather than scene (SPorts Movies, Training, Fast table making)
9. Match Cuts - Match action from one scene to another  (Jumping on Bed to Jumping in sea)
